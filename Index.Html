ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Jess, an Independant Consultant with The Body Shop at Home. I started this journey in 2020 to help others and keep myself occupied during Covid. I loved the products after a friend bought me some for Christmas and started purchasing from a Body Shop at Home consultant. She asked me whether I wanted to start a family and friends account and that's how Bee Harmony was born. After a while, customers asked about candles and wax melts as well. I went to a candle party last year and loved the principles, ethics and local small business of Matty's candles and asked to add them to my portfolio. Fast forward to the end of 2022 and I've re-launched Bee Harmony as The Three B's. Firstly, once I launched Bee Harmony, it sounded too much like EHarmony - eek! It did however show a love of products with yourself though. The Three B's I chose for a number of reasons - firstly my school nickname was Jess Bee but then I liked the idea that this could support a relaxing experience at home for your body and mind, through beauty and bath products.
I would love to help in any way I can, whether it is something for you, a loved one or a precious gift. I can help pick the perfect item, wrap it and deliver at as well. I can help as much or as little as you would like, with no pressure at all.
Have a great day! Jess

MATTY'S CANDLES

Matty's candles are a small independent company that use Coconut and Rapeseed wax and recycled packaging to make the perfect long lasting, scented sustainable candles. For Matty's candles feel free to browse the website and brochure, then drop me a message to order. There is so much on offer from wax melts, candles, accessories to diffusers, in scents that will blow you away as there is something for everyone to love. They also last forever too!

THE BODY SHOP AT HOMETM

The Body Shop is a company that many people will recognise for its range of bath, body and beauty products since 1976. The Body Shop at Home was born in 1994, allowing independent consultants to support customers directly from home. There is so much to offer from skincare, body care, hair care, make-up and beyond that is ethically produced, supports workers with a fairtrade wage and has a sustainable agenda to support the planet. There are multiple ways to order - you can use the link in the banner at the top to order at home on your own direct to you, I can create a basket for you, or we can sort ordering to delivery face to face. It is entirely up to you!
